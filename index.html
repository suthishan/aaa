Sri Kollapuri Amman Temple

This temple is maintained by private family for many years, since I am visiting this temple from early 1970s onwards. A powerful Goddess, we pray for our family well-being. During Tamil month Aadi (around August 10-15) Aadi uthsavam will be celebrated with pomp and gaiety, with daily special abishegam, arathana, free feeding (annnadhanam), music etc.
